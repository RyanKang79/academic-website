Biography

Sung-il Kang is a PhD candidate and distinguished practitioner with over 21 years of experience in small and medium enterprise (SME) finance and credit guarantee systems. Currently serving as Deputy General Manager at Korea Credit Guarantee Fund's Corporate Improvement Division, he brings a unique combination of academic rigor and practical expertise to his research.

His research focuses on dynamic capabilities, entrepreneurship, and financial innovation, with particular emphasis on how organizations adapt and thrive in rapidly changing environments. His work bridges the gap between theoretical frameworks and real-world applications, drawing from his extensive experience in policy implementation and corporate restructuring.

As a PhD candidate in Convergence Consulting at Daejeon University, he continues to contribute to academic knowledge while maintaining his commitment to practical problem-solving in the financial sector. His research has been published in leading journals and has received recognition from academic and professional organizations.
