Research Interests

Dynamic Capabilities

Investigating how organizations develop and deploy dynamic capabilities to achieve sustainable competitive advantage in rapidly changing environments.

SME Finance

Analyzing credit guarantee systems and their impact on small and medium enterprise growth, recovery, and access to finance.

Entrepreneurship

Exploring startup ecosystems, entrepreneurial orientation, and innovation processes in emerging markets, particularly in Asia.

Corporate Restructuring

Examining organizational recovery strategies, business model innovation, and the role of government support in corporate turnarounds.

FinTech Innovation

Studying financial technology adoption, digital transformation in financial services, and regulatory implications.

Sustainable Strategy

Researching proactive sustainable strategies, eco-innovation, and the role of sensing capabilities in environmental management.
