Research

Advancing knowledge in dynamic capabilities, SME finance, and entrepreneurship through interdisciplinary research

Research Areas

Dynamic Capabilities

Investigating how organizations sense, seize, and reconfigure resources to achieve sustained competitive advantage in rapidly changing environments. Focus on capability development in Asian SMEs and the role of organizational learning.

Sensing Capabilities Resource Reconfiguration Organizational Learning Competitive Advantage

SME Finance & Credit Guarantee

Analyzing the effectiveness of credit guarantee systems in supporting small and medium enterprises. Research on financial inclusion, risk assessment, and the impact of government-backed financing programs on business growth.

Credit Guarantee Financial Inclusion Risk Assessment Government Policy

Entrepreneurship & Innovation

Exploring entrepreneurial orientation, startup ecosystems, and innovation processes in emerging markets. Special focus on the role of institutional support and cultural factors in fostering entrepreneurial activities.

Entrepreneurial Orientation Startup Ecosystem Innovation Management Emerging Markets

Corporate Restructuring & Recovery

Examining organizational recovery strategies, business model innovation, and the effectiveness of restructuring programs. Research on distressed firm rehabilitation and the role of stakeholder coordination.

Corporate Recovery Business Model Innovation Restructuring Stakeholder Management

FinTech & Digital Innovation

Studying the adoption of financial technology, digital transformation in financial services, and the regulatory implications of fintech innovations. Focus on emerging market contexts and SME digitalization.

FinTech Adoption Digital Transformation Regulatory Technology SME Digitalization

Sustainable Strategy & ESG

Investigating proactive sustainable strategies, eco-innovation, and Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) practices in organizations. Research on the relationship between sustainability and firm performance.

Sustainable Strategy Eco-Innovation ESG Practices Environmental Management
